Roles For those who prefer to have a few additional skills, we have a roles permission system
Available roles

If you are interested in purchasing any of these roles, please contact our staff via contact@oznet.fun or through our Discord server. Payments can be made using PayPal, credit card, or cryptocurrency. Our staff will guide you through the process and activate your role once the payment is confirmed.

Players can get one classic role and one advanced role.

Default role
Average permission level
[Villager] Price: Free Advantages: Can set one home, and teleport to this home, can claim and protect regions for their homes. Default role for everyone!
Classic roles
Average permission level

All classic roles can set up to three homes and teleport back to the place where they died. Players can only choose one of the classic roles but not combine multiple classic roles.

[Builder] Price: 5€ / month Advantages: Unlimited stone, very efficient everlasting axe, and more to come!
[Miner] Price: 5€ / month Advantages: Very efficient everlasting pickaxe, ability to mine mob spawners, no damage from falling, and more to come!
[Blacksmith] Price: 5€ / month Advantages: Can repair everything forever without resources, can apply some low-level enhancements without resources, and more to come!
[Cook] Price: 5€ / month Advantages: Unlimited coal, one stack of golden apples per month, and more to come!
[Hunter] Price: 10€ / month Advantages: Super enhanced everlasting bow with unlimited arrows, super enhanced full diamond armor and sword, can instantly heal once every hour, and more to come!
Advanced roles
Average permission level

All advanced roles can set up to five homes and teleport back to the place where they died. Players can only choose one of the advanced roles but not combine multiple advanced roles.

[Sorcerer] Price: 20€ / month Advantages: Access to magical powers (thunder, fireballs...), can sight teleport, unlimited offensive potions, and more to come!
[Druid] Price: 20€ / month Advantages: Can heal and remove hunger for themselves and others, can see in the dark, can summon animals and monsters, and more to come!
[Alchemist] Price: 20€ / month Advantages: Unlimited potions of any kind, unlimited gold, and more to come!
[Death knight] Price: 40€ / month Advantages: Super enhanced full stuff, exclusive mount, can fly, can walk on water, can move x3 speed, can summon monsters, and more to come!
Special roles
Average permission level

All special roles can set unlimited homes, teleport back to the place where they died, teleport to any player or coordinate. All these roles are given only after reviewing candidate's application.

[Peacekeeper] Position: Open Advantages: Can instantly kill anyone, access the inventory of anyone, access claimed regions, activate fly and god mode, and move at 10x speed.

They receive instructions from the [Judge].
[Judge] Position: Open Advantages: Can kick, ban and mute players in case of abuse, can sanction players after receiving a complaint, can activate fly and god mode.

They mandate the [Peacekeeper] to carry out the sanction.
Staff roles
Average permission level

All staff roles can activate creative mode. All these roles are given only after reviewing candidate's application.

[Game Master] Position: Open Advantages: Can operate the server, manage users, manage roles, moderate the chat and players behaviour, help [Admin] administrating the server.
[Admin] Position: Closed Advantages: Administrates the server, plugins, and high level management. Receives application for and manages the [Game Master].
